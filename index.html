Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15-12-2024

Welcome to Bulk Link Opener! This privacy policy explains how we handle your data, the permissions used in the extension, and your rights as a user.

1. Features of the Extension

  • Allows users to input multiple URLs and open them simultaneously in new tabs or new browser windows.
  • Provides settings such as:
    • Open URLs in new tabs or new windows.
    • Limit the number of URLs to open.
    • Remember the URL list for future sessions.
    • Remove opened URLs from the input list.
  • Includes a Dark Mode toggle to improve the user interface experience.

2. Data Collection and Usage

The extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information beyond what is required for its functionality.

  • Local Storage: URLs entered in the extension are stored locally on your browser if the "Remember the URL List" setting is enabled.
  • Permissions Used:
    • tabs: To open links in new tabs or windows.
    • storage: To save user settings and preferences.
    • host_permissions: Allows the extension to open any HTTP/HTTPS URL provided by the user.
  • Clipboard: The extension accesses the clipboard only when the user manually pastes or copies links. This access is triggered by user action.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third-party services or individuals.

4. Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can clear all stored URLs at any time using the "Clear" button.
  • If you uninstall the extension, all locally stored data will be removed automatically.

5. Third-Party Services

The extension does not use or integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
